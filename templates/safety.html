How do we look after our user's safety?

Our carpooling app prioritizes safety by implementing a rigorous authentication process, including Aadhar number verification, to ensure a secure and trustworthy community of users. Additionally, we provide essential details such as age, gender, and ratings of potential co-passengers, empowering users to make informed decisions and choose companions that match their preferences. This commitment to safety and transparency creates a reliable and trustworthy carpooling environment, fostering trust and community among our users, and ensuring their peace of mind throughout their journey.
